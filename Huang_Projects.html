Image Manipulation

(3.6.18 - 3.26.18)

For this project, the assignment that we were given was to create a project that involves manipulating an image according to the demands of the consumer. All of these features could be customized any way that they wished. So they could choose whatever option they would want for their political image. In addition, I was assigned with designing and the final product. This was a challenging task, considering the fact that there were many design obstacles that was needed to be overcome, such as sizing, safety, and being able to be adopted to certain enviorments.
